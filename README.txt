Arizona AI Transition Pathways Website Prototype

Files included:
- index.html
- styles.css
- app.js
- data.js

How to use:
1. Unzip the folder.
2. Open index.html in a browser.
3. Update data.js with your own occupation, labor market, transition, and Arizona training data.

Suggested next upgrades:
- Replace seeded data with O*NET + state labor market estimates.
- Add CIP-to-SOC crosswalk driven training results.
- Add occupation pages or router-based navigation.
- Add export, comparison, and county filters.
